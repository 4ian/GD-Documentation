    gdjs.Layer Class

    Defined in: ../Runtime/layer.js:7

    Represents a layer used to display objects.

    The layer connects its Pixi container to the Pixi stage during its construction, but then it is objects responsibility to connect themselves to the layer's container (see addChildToPIXIContainer method).

    Layers do not provide direct access to their pixi container as they do some extra work to ensure that z orders remains correct.

    Not yet implemented: Viewports and support for multiple cameras
